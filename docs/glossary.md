# Glossary

## Air Region

A map area where air wings can be assigned to missions such as air superiority or close air support.

## Civilian Factory

A building used to construct other buildings, repair damage, and trade for resources.

## Close Air Support

Aircraft support that helps ground troops during land battles.

## Military Factory

A building used to produce land and air equipment, such as rifles, artillery, trucks, tanks, and planes.

## National Focus

A country-specific decision path that gives political, industrial, military, or diplomatic benefits.

## Production Line

A manufacturing queue where military factories produce a specific type of equipment.

## Supply Hub

A logistics point that distributes supply to nearby divisions.
